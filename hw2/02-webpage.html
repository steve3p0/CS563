The Portland Rosebuds

1914 - 1918, 1925 - 1926

The Portland Rosebuds were the first American hockey team to hold the Stanley Cup in 1916. The team suspended operations in 1918, but played one last season in 1925-26. The team was moved to Chicago and became the Chicago Blackhawks.

The Portland Buckaroos

1928 - 1941, 1960 - 1975

The Portland Buckaroos were the second professional ice hockey team in Portland Oregon. They struggled during their early years (1928-1941), but eventually found their footing in the 1960s and early 70s. The won league championships in 1965 and 1971.

The Portland Winterhawks

1976 - Current

The Portland Winterhaws are a junior ice hockey team and were the first American team to win the Canadian Hockey Leaugue championship in 1983. The team has won multiple championships since then and has over 6,000 players drafted by the NHL.
